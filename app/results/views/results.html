  • Mojito

    You have everything!

    Description

    A traditional Cuban drink, its combination of sweetness, refreshing citrus, and mint flavors is intended to complement the potent kick of the rum, and has made this clear highball a popular summer drink.

    Needed

    • White rum
    • Lime juice

    Preparation

    • Add lime juice to sugar and mint leaves
    • Add rum and stir
    • Top it off with ice cubes and sparkling soda water
